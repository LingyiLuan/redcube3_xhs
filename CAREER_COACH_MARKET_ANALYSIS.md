## üéØ B2B Opportunity: Selling to Career Coaches

### **Value Proposition for Coaches:**

**"Give your clients data-driven interview intelligence, not just generic advice"**

**Benefits:**
1. **Save Time:** Coaches don't need to research each company manually
2. **Better Results:** Data-driven insights vs. generic advice
3. **Scale:** Serve more clients with same time investment
4. **Competitive Edge:** Offer insights competitors don't have
5. **Client Retention:** Better outcomes = happier clients

### **Pricing Models for Coaches:**

#### **Option 1: Per-Coach Subscription** (Like Big Interview)
- **Price:** $99-199/month per coach
- **Includes:** Unlimited client access, coach dashboard, white-label options
- **Target:** Independent career coaches (1-5 clients/month)

#### **Option 2: Per-Client Pricing** (Like Big Interview)
- **Price:** $20-30/month per client
- **Includes:** Full platform access for each client
- **Target:** Coaches with many clients (10-50 clients)

#### **Option 3: Enterprise/Team Pricing**
- **Price:** $500-2,000/month for coaching firms
- **Includes:** Multiple coaches, unlimited clients, white-label, API access
- **Target:** Career coaching companies (10+ coaches)

#### **Option 4: Usage-Based Pricing**
- **Price:** $0.50-1.00 per analysis/report generated
- **Includes:** Pay per use, no monthly fee
- **Target:** Coaches with variable client load
